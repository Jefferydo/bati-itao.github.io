Achievements

  • ICT accessibility CoP event held on Oct. 25 on the topic of ITAO site and resources.
  • ESDC Self-Paced Web Accessibility Course was presented at the Access Working Group (interdepartmental working groups).
  • ACE presentations of five portfolios/disabilities categories in 10 sessions in both English and French.
  • ACE shipped accessible keyboards and audio cables to all Service Canada Centres (316 keyboards and 2644 USB cables).
  • ACE completed the releases/upgrades for the following software:
    • Jaws 2022
    • Fusion 2021
    • Colour Contrast Analyser 3.1.4
    • WordQ Pro Frech 5
    • LiveScribe + 1.8.4 upgrade
  • ACE completed Windows 10 21H2 testing for adaptive technology.
  • ACE completed usability testing for IT Alerts and reported issues to the implementation team.

Challenges

  • Integrating desktop accessibility into the RMP.
  • Ability to test for Authoring Tool Accessibility Guidelines is limited due to workload and priority constraints
  • Delays implementing AT software to employees with disabilities because of the complexity of the IT Security assessment during the OCMC process.
  • Bottleneck in packaging AT software because of the lack of capacity in the packaging team.
  • Revised ICT Accessibility strategy and alignment with ESDC Accessibility Office (EAO) strategy.
  • ACP received a small percentage of responses on the current and future product accessibility plans from vendors. The information is required to complete the ACP roadmap.
  • ACE is working with an ESDC group (Quebec region) in charge of hiring employees with disabilities and working to solve various challenges related to hardware purchase.

Roadblocks

  • Funding for ACP project for next five years.
  • Engaging Application Development team and the COTS owners for their commitments to work on achieving compliance due to conflicting priorities.
